Who we are

Sri Ramakrishna Kudil, founded in 1949 at Thirupparaithurai, is a charitable residential home dedicated to supporting orphaned and underprivileged children. With over seven decades of service, the Kudil continues to provide food, shelter, education, discipline, and life-skills training in a nurturing spiritual environment.

  • Our Mission

    To offer children a safe home filled with love, education, and moral guidance—empowering them to become confident, independent, and responsible citizens.

  • Our Vision

    To create a community where every child, regardless of background, receives equal opportunity to grow through education, discipline, self-reliance, and spiritual values.

  • Our Value

    Our values are rooted in compassion, discipline, simplicity, community living, education, and self-reliance—guiding every child to grow with responsibility, gratitude, and dignity.
